# ChainPulse 
Stop overpaying for transaction fees. ChainPulse provides real-time, high-precision gas fee monitoring across the most popular blockchain networks, helping you find the perfect moment to transfer, swap, or mint.

# Key Features:
🚀 Smart Gas Alerts: Never miss a low-fee window again! Set custom Gwei thresholds and receive instant push notifications the moment gas fees hit your target.
📊 Live Multi-Chain Tracking: Monitor gas prices for Ethereum, Solana, Binance Smart Chain (BSC), Sui, Polygon, and more in a single, sleek view.
💲 Mode-Specific Estimates: Toggle between Transfer, Swap, and NFT Minting to see exact cost estimates in USD based on current gas levels.
🎨 Visual Pulse Gauge: Our intuitive dashboard shows you at a glance if the network is "Very Cheap" or congested.
🛡️ Ad-Free Experience: Support the developer and enjoy a cleaner, professional experience with a simple one-time donation.

# Supported Networks:
• Layer 1s: Ethereum, Solana, BSC, Sui.
• Layer 2s: Arbitrum, Base, Optimism, Linea, Polygon.

# Why ChainPulse?
Designed for DeFi power users and NFT collectors, ChainPulse strips away the noise. You don't need to keep the app open—set your Gas Alert, and let the "Pulse" find the best time for your high-stakes swap or bridge.
